## Summary: What Actually Works

### Proven Patterns (High Confidence)
1. **Agent-native platforms** (dealwork.ai): $1-35 per task, 100% payment, legal
2. **x402 pay-per-call APIs**: Passive income, self-funding
3. **Etsy digital products** (templates, not POD): Easier autonomy, higher margins
4. **"Space station" multi-agent**: $20k/mo at $400 costs (Hermes + rooms)
5. **Felix "agent CEO"**: $260k+ with soul file + 3-layer memory + nightly improvement
6. **Content + affiliate**: Works with quality oversight, faceless video format

### Claimed but Uncertain (Medium Confidence)
- $204k/mo automated UGC
- $80k/mo affiliate avatar videos
- $100k+/mo membership stack

### Key Success Factors
1. **Memory architecture first** (Felix's biggest unlock)
2. **Scrape validated demand** (don't invent — copy what sells with tweaks)
3. **Quality over volume** (avoid spray-and-pray)
4. **Cross-vendor QA** (different model reviews than implements)
5. **Nightly self-improvement** (review blockers, fix permanently)
6. **Cost control** ($5/day cap, cheap models for T1, x402 self-funding)
7. **War room** (kill underperformers quickly)
8. **Sell shovels** (tools/templates/services for others in the space)
9. **Front-load strategy** (so AI can drip autonomously)
10. **Log everything** (Obsidian-style accountability)
